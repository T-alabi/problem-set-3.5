Important People


Friedrich Ludwig Jahn

Friedrich Ludwig jhan is known as the father of gymnastics because he made the base of modern day gymnastics equiptment. the equiptment he developed made gymnastics into a more formal sport that was to be taken seriosuly.

Morinari Watanabe

Morinari Watanabe is a japenese Businessman who has been serving as the president of the International Gymnastics federation since Janurary 2017. Before he served as the IGF, Watanabe served as the general secutary of the Japan gymnastics assosciation.
